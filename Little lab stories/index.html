Taman Imajinasi Anak Semesta (TIAS)

Taman Imajinasi Anak Semesta (TIAS) is a creative sanctuary designed to nurture children’s imagination in a fast-paced, digital world. Through nature-inspired mixed media art, storytelling, and hands-on exploration, TIAS helps children express themselves, connect with their environment, and experience creativity as a joyful journey, not just the act of making art.

Online Story Listening Class

with Ms. Laura

Welcome to a gentle and joyful online story listening class, where children learn English naturally through storytelling as a listening-based method.
Guided by Ms. Laura, children are invited to slow down, listen with care, and enter a world of imagination. Through carefully guided storytelling, children focus on listening while naturally absorbing English sounds, words, and expressions without pressure or expectation to perform.
This class uses storytelling as a method to build strong listening foundations, support simple interaction, and deepen understanding. It is not about memorizing or speaking perfectly. It is about listening, understanding, and enjoying English through stories.
Designed for young learners who are just beginning or growing their English, each session feels calm, playful, and meaningful, helping children build confidence through listening.

Ms. Laura

Laura is an experienced education professional based in Jakarta, Indonesia, with over a decade of service as both an ESL teacher at ACG School Jakarta and a lecturer at Universitas Paramadina, positions she has held since 2014. She holds a strong academic background in communications, including a Master's in Business/Corporate Communications from Fairleigh Dickinson University, which complements her prior brief experience in marketing, relocation, and corporate communication fields.

Mr. BirdFlute

TIAS is founded by Rangga Firmansyah, also known as Mr. Birdflut a writer, educator, game researcher, and storyteller whose work blends language, culture, and art in poetic ways. He is also the founder of littlelabstories.com. With a deep passion for mindful, nature-based creativity, Rangga creates gentle spaces where children can slow down, imagine, and explore through the beauty of natural patterns, textures, and expressive drawing methods such as his FloLines EmoGraph approach.
